The Art of the Perfect Espresso

Posted by Ngh Laren on October 20, 2025

The view from the window overlooking the trees and the small road is also very relaxing. The shop also has cats, seeing them dozing on the balcony is so charming, Friendly staff, quick orders. Cheap prices compared to the space and vibe. I find the prices cheaper than other study cafes I've been to, but there's not a variety of dishes. For me, I usually drink those drinks so no problem. 10 points, I will definitely come back
